Success Implementation Checklist

Follow this checklist to ensure your resume is perfectly optimized and ready for submission

  • Preparation: Gather all relevant experience, dates, and achievements. Research target job posting and identify key requirements/keywords.
  • Content Creation: Customize all [brackets] with your specific, quantified information. Focus on results and impact. Use industry keywords naturally and replace weak verbs with powerful Action Verbs.
  • Quantification: Verify all achievements are quantified with numbers, percentages, or dollar signs.
  • ATS Check: Ensure simple, clean formatting and proper keyword integration.
  • Consistency Check: Verify verb tense, punctuation, and style are consistent across all sections.
  • Format: Save your final document as a PDF to preserve formatting.
